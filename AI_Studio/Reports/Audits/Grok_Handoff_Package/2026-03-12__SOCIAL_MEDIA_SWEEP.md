# Social Media Sweep — Claude Code Community Sentiment Analysis
**Date**: March 12, 2026 | **Conducted by**: Claude Code (6 parallel search agents)

## Executive Summary

120+ unique sources across 8 platforms. Instagram is a dead zone for complaints. Twitter/X and LinkedIn are the richest sources. The DataTalksClub DB wipe is the single most covered incident (10+ outlets). Sentiment trajectory: enthusiastic adoption (mid-2025) → significant frustration (late 2025 through early 2026).
