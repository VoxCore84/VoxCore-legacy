What Are These Files?
=====================

These are parsed packet captures from your WoW sessions.
WowPacketParser converted your Ymir .pkt captures into readable text and SQL.

  .txt files  — Human-readable packet data (every network message decoded)
  .sql files  — Database queries extracted from the packets

For CreatureCodex specifically:
  - The addon already captured creature spells while you were playing
  - Open the addon in-game (/cc) and click Export Data for ready-to-use SQL
  - These WPP files are the raw source data if you want to dig deeper

To generate creature_template_spell SQL, use the addon's in-game export
(/cc export) or run tools/wpp_import.py --sql on your WPP output.
